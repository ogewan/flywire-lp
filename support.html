Support

Technical or scientific questions related to proofreading should be posted in the Forum.

General inquiries: email flywire@princeton.edu

FlyWire Forum

When you sign up for FlyWire, you will receive an invitation to join the user forum at FlyWire Forum, where you can ask questions or answer other people’s questions. Sign up for notifications if you’d like to be an active participant in the community.

Asking for help validating your choices

You can ask for validation from other community users before you commit an edit.

  • If it’s a merge you’re trying to do, make sure both pieces are turned on in color. Right click on the location where they touch to center it.
  • If it’s a split you’re trying to do, center the image where you think the split should occur. Shorten the URL using the Up arrow (top right of screen)
  • Copy the URL and paste it into the FlyWire Forum channel #help_proofreading.
  • Add a mention of whether you’re trying to do a split or merge.
